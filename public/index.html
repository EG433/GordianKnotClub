What Our Club Does
As a student-run hedge fund, we aim to stimulate real-world asset management experience for UCSD students by employing option integrated investment strategies that balance risk and growth beyond textbook knowledge, empowering members with practical skills in risk assessment and investment.
Portfolio Investment
Asset allocation, Option, Valuation
The portfolio investment at Gordian Knot Capital is committed to building a portfolio across a wide range of asset types. By leveraging a mix of equities, bonds, REITs, commodities, cryptocurrencies, and country-level macro investments, we aim to optimize returns while managing risk. Each asset class is paired with tailored options strategies to enhance performance under the supervision of the risk department to achieve our investment goals.
Alternative Investment
Cryptocurrency, Short-swing trade
The Alternative Investments at Gordian Knot Capital is dedicated to exploring high-risk, high-reward opportunities beyond traditional asset classes. This department focuses on dynamic and innovative investment strategies, including cryptocurrency, short swing trades based on short-term signals, and leveraging financial release dates to capitalize on market movements.
Risk Management
VaR, Sharpe ratio, Volatility
We introduce detailed risk profiles, incorporating metrics like the Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown, and Value at Risk (VaR), to help clients understand the risk associated with their investments. A significant challenge has been to treat assets and their corresponding options positions as a unified entity in these calculations.
Operations
Advertisement, Website maintenance, data visualizations
Support the club's strategic objectives through robust operational processes, high-quality data visualization, and dynamic online presence management. This department is responsible for handling various critical functions such as advertisement, hiring, website maintenance, and visualizing asset management performance.
Professional Knowledge
CFA, FRM, DCF, Competitions
We provide a structured curriculum covering portfolio theory, risk management, and professional certifications, with experienced members sharing their CFA and FRM insights. Practical lessons include guided asset management and strategic decision-making using models like the Gordon Growth Model and DCF. Additionally, we lead our team in participating in stock pitch, private equity, and quantitative competitions hosted by renowned institutions like UBS.
